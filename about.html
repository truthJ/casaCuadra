ABOUT

casa cuadra

Casa Cuadra is a Miami-based online shop featuring a collection of modern vintage, second hand and contemporary pieces for you and your home.

  • Small moments in everyday life are gathered to make life. Our products are gathered to create your daily life and your space.
  • That's why even though it's a vintage product, we try to showcase it with the best design and best quality. From product selection to meticulous packaging, we do everything perfectly.
  • Casa Cuadra strives to melt beauty and speciality into your life, daily life and space.
