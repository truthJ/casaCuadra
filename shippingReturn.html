POLICIES

Shipping & Returns

Domestic Orders

  • All orders are shipped from Miami, Florida, Monday through Friday within 2-3 business days of order confirmation.
  • Orders placed from Friday-Sunday or on holidays will ship the following week.
  • A shipment confirmation email that includes a tracking number will be sent once your package has been mailed.
  • If you would like expedited shipping, please email us at hi@casacuadra.com prior to placing your order and we will add the expedited shipping rate to your purchase.
  • Casa Cuadra is not responsible for delays in shipping or delivery due to force of nature or other uncontrollable events.
  • We cannot ship to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO/DPO addresses.

Lost/Stolen/Damaged Packages

  • Casa Cuadra is not responsible for lost or stolen packages, so we highly recommend using a delivery address that is secure.
  • All packages are insured with the shipping carrier and it is the customer’s responsibility to file a claim with the carrier if a package arrives damaged or goes missing.

International Orders

  • International shipments depend largely upon the pace with which they are processed through customs.
  • We are unable to control or predict delivery windows for these packages.
  • Duties and taxes are not included in the total at checkout. They are determined by the customs agency within the destination country.
  • Although charges are not always incurred, assessment of duties and taxes will be based on the value of the order and the tax-free threshold, if it exists, for goods imported into the destination country.
  • Payment of any duties and import taxes is the responsibility of the recipient, and these fees are collected at the time of delivery.
  • Refused packages are still liable for any and all customs fees and will incur a 20% restocking fee.
  • We are not responsible for these fees or delays, and suggest customers contact their local customs bureau with inquiries or to see if your package is being held locally.

Returns

  • All items are final sale. No returns or exchanges.
  • Vintage items are one of a kind and sold as is. Due to their age and previous wear, some items may have slight imperfections, please keep it in mind when making your purchase.
  • If you have any questions regarding an item, please email us at hi@casacuadra.com.
